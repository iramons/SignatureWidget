Privacy Policy

Signature Widgets

← Back to home

Effective date: June 5, 2025

The short version: Signature Widgets does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Your signatures stay on your device. No accounts, no analytics, no third-party SDKs.

1. Information We Collect

Signature Widgets does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No name, email address, or contact information
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No usage analytics or crash reports sent externally
  • No location data
  • No camera or microphone access

2. Data Stored on Your Device

All data created within the app — including your drawn signatures, stroke data, colors, and widget configuration — is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage mechanisms (SwiftData and App Group shared storage for widgets).

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

3. iCloud & Backups

Signature Widgets does not use iCloud sync. Your signatures may be included in your device's standard iTunes/iCloud backup, which is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

4. In-App Purchases

Signature Widgets offers an optional subscription to unlock full access. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple via the App Store. We never receive or store your payment details.

5. Third-Party Services

Signature Widgets does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics services. The app operates entirely on-device.

6. Children's Privacy

Signature Widgets does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app complies with COPPA and GDPR requirements.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update the effective date above and, if the changes are significant, notify users through an app update notice.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on GitHub or reach out via the App Store support link.
